This is a pathfinding visualizer and maze generator I wrote for my lectures related to data structures. It is written in ReactJS. 

Maze generation algorithms:
* Recursive Division

Pathfinding algorithms:
* Depth First Search
* Breadth First Search
* Dijkstra
* A Star
